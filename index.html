Threats & Mitigations

We see the risks. Here's how we counter them.

Political – Harm Reduction Headwinds
Government pressure threatens customer budgets. Mitigation: channel diversification across institutional, distributor, and D2C reduces single-source dependency. Our commercial viability is not grant-dependent.
Legal / Liability
Emergency medication cases carry liability risk if protection fails. Mitigation: our engineering-first approach – waterproofing, impact resistance, single-body design – ensures medication integrity. This is a defensible moat, not just a feature.
🥊 Competition – Nove
Nove's smaller size was their only edge. Mitigation: v2.0 is 35% lighter and 25% smaller – eliminating their advantage while strengthening every dimension we already owned.
